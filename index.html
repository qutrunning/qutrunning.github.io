About

QUT Running is a social & fitness group for anyone interested in improving their health, fitness and well-being. We are by no means elite runners, but cater to people at any level from beginner to the very experienced. Being a social club we also host a number of events throughout the year, and provide you with the opportunity to meet a variety of new people at QUT.

Signing up

Just show up either at our O-Week stall or at one of our sessions throughout the semester and talk to a member of our Exec Team. We're happy to answer any questions you may have. QUT Running Social

QUT Running has a big social aspect. Following Monday's session, we unwind with dinner and drinks at Beach House in the city. This is a great way to meet new friends thanks to a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. After dinner we kick on at Music City for karaoke and then head to the Down Under bar. We have a few drinks at both places and enter teams in Bar Wars, which is always massive fun. Every few weeks we spice things up, by booking out a couple of lanes at Strike Bowling and playing laser tag, or having pizza and drinks on the lawn at South Bank.

We also attend events hosted by the QUT Guild and co-host events with other clubs, such as QUT Snowriders and QUT Cheer and Dance. These provide excellent opportunities to meet people from outside QUT Running in an a relaxed environment!

Now that you have all the info, come and join us on Monday evening after our running session! We look forward to seeing you! Don't forget to join the Facebook group for more information.
