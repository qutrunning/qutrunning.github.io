ABOUT








QUT Running is a social & fitness group for anyone interested in improving their health, fitness and well-being. We are by no means elite runners, but cater to people at any level from beginner to the very experienced. Being a social club we also host a number of events throughout the year, and provide you with the opportunity to meet a variety of new people at QUT. We believe everyone can love running. So if you want to start up, pick up where you left off or just keep going this is the perfect group for you. We tailor our sessions to be fun, sociable, welcoming and at the same time be providing great fitness oportunities.

Signing up

Do you want to become a member of QUT Running and QUT Running Social? Just show up either at our O-Week stall or at one of our sessions throughout the semester and talk to a member of our Exec Team. We are happy to answer any and all questions and sign you up on the spot. You can even try a running session before you join, free of charge. IMPORTANT: Please ensure that you come around early enough, so that we have time to talk to you without delaying our session. Within the first few weeks the Exec Team will always be at the Kidney Lawn 30 min prior to the start of the session.

QUT Running Social


QUT Running has a big social aspect. Following our running session on Monday we unwind with dinner and drinks at Beach House in the city. This is a great way to get to know your fellow runners, as it is a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. After dinner we usually kick on at Music City for karaoke and then head to the Down Under bar. We have a few drinks at both places and enter teams in Bar Wars, which is always massive fun. Every few weeks we spice things up, by booking out a couple of lanes at Strike Bowling and playing laser tag, or having pizza and drinks on the lawn at South Bank.

We also attend events hosted by the QUT Guild and co-host events with other clubs, such as QUT Snowriders and QUT Cheer and Dance. These provide excellent opportunities to meet people from outside QUT Running in an a relaxed environment!

Now that you have a bit of a background on the social side of the club, come and join us on Monday evening after our running session! We look forward to seeing you! Don't forget to join the Facebook group for more information.


EVENTS AND RACES

Twilight Running Festival
QUT Running will be entering teams into various running and obstacle races throughout the year. One of the earliest races of the year is the Twilight running festival held at St Lucia on the 20th March. This is a festival with distances suiting everyone with tracks spanning 21.1km, 10km, 5km and 1km in length.

QUT Classic
QUT Classic is an event organised by the Guild and is set on the 15 of May every year. The distances covered are 10km, 5km and 1km.

Brisbane Marathon
Brisbane Marathon Festival is one of Brisbane’s major sporting events. The distances covered are 42.2km, 21.1km, 10km, 5km and the courses are run through the CBD on the 7th of August.

Bridge to Brisbane
Later on in August (28th), the annual Bridge to Brisbane event occurs. The race distances are 10km and 5km – with the 10km starting at the Gateway Bridge and finishing at the Brisbane RNA Showgrounds. QUT Running always has a strong presence in this event.

Tough Mudder
As well as running events, QUT running usually enters teams in obstacle races such as Tough Mudder (7th & 8th May) and Spartan Race (27th Feb) also. These events are always a lot of fun and can be taken as seriously or laid-back as you wish.

Other Events
There are many other events happing through the year in Brisbane and we will aim to get involved in as many as possible. If you have any suggestions of events you’d like to participate in, let one of our executives know and we can organise a team!

OVERVIEW

Swimming (FM)


Is there any better motivation to start into a new week than to jump into a pool? Of course there isn’t! So come along to our Monday morning swimming session. The meeting point is right in front of the Healthstream entrance next to the food court at P Block on Gardens Point Campus. Please be there shortly before 8am so that you don’t lose too much time getting changed. Using 2 different 50m olympic-length swimming lanes, we provide good exercises and advice for your entire body, catered to your individual skill level. Full membership is required!



Running


This is our most popular running session. Regardless of your running skills, come along to a fun session where we run through the relaxing Botanic Gardens. Our running coordinator Eamon is able to demonstrate and coordinate many different exercises to help you to improve your agility, speed, acceleration, and endurance. The sessions are usually in the form of an interval session with varying length and amounts of the intervals. Furthermore, this is a great opportunity to meet other fellow runners, to have a chat or to just make friends. If you are new to QUT Running, this is the session you should definitely attend. We are happy to answer questions, explain what’s going on or even introduce you to other people. Meeting point for this session is the Kidney Lawn. From there we head to X block to store our bags before our run.



Casual Running


Not able to attend our Monday sesh? Hungover? No worries! This casual running session is suitable for everybody. In this particular session, we take it easier today than our Monday afternoon runs, and start at 8:30am from Kidney Lawn on Fridays. It is then held at either within the Botanic Gardens or around and along the river.


Gym (FM)


Healthstream has provided us with the opportunity for us to use their gym facilities (including changing room and showers) on Wednesdays from 3pm to 4pm. Meeting point is right in front of the Healthstream entrance next to the food court at P block on Gardens Point Campus. Please be there shortly before 3pm, so that you don’t lose too much time getting changed. During the gym session you are your own boss, we won’t tell you what to do. Create your own workout or take some advice from our experienced gym coordinator Joe. Full membership is required!



Beginner Running


Our Beginner session on Thursdays at 4pm is for all those who have recently started to run or are coming back from a longer break. All exercises are designed for beginners! We help you to get into running as a sport or hobby and assist in motivating you to run more often. We won’t cover a long distance or a high speed. Meeting point is the Kidney Lawn.



Bootcamp


Bootcamp is an excellent opportunity to hone your stamina and strength/stability at the same time. Our bootcamp coordinator Joe combines running and body-weight exercises for you so that you can get that perfect beach body! Every fitness level is welcome. Meeting point is the Kidney Lawn.
