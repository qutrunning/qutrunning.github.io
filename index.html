About QUT Running

QUT Running is a social & fitness group, for anyone interested in improving their health, fitness and well-being. We are by no means elite runners, but cater to people at any level from beginner to the very experienced. Being a social club we also host a number of events throughout the year, and give you the opportunity to meet a variety of new people at QUT. We believe everyone can love running. So if you want to start up, pick up where you left off or just keep going this is the perfect group for you. We taylor our sessions to be fun, sociable and welcoming at the same time providing great fitness oportunities.

Join our group on Facebook - https://www.facebook.com/groups/qutrunning/


Signing up

You want to become a member of QUT Running and QUT Running Social? Just show up either on our O-Week stall or on one of our sessions throughout the semester and talk to our Exec Team. We are happy to answer questions and sign you up on the spot. You can even try a running session before you join. IMPORTANT: Please ensure that you come around early enough, so that we have time to talk to you without delaying our session. Within the first few weeks the Exec Team will always be at the Kidney Lawn 30 min prior to the start of the session.

QUT Running Social

QUT Running has a big social aspect. Following our running session on Monday we unwind with dinner and drinks at Beach House in the city. This is a great way to get to know your fellow runners, as it is a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. After dinner we kick on at Music City for karaoke and then onto Down Under. We have a few drinks at both places and enter teams in Bar Wars, which is always massive fun. Every few weeks we spice things up, by booking out a couple of lanes at Strike Bowling and playing laser tag, or having pizza and drinks on the lawn at South Bank.

We also attend events hosted by the QUT Guild and co-host events with other clubs, such as QUT Snowriders and Cheer and Dance. These also provide excellent opportunities to meet people from outside QUT Running!

Now that you have a bit of a background on the social side of the club, come and join us on Monday evening after our running session! We look forward to seeing you! Join the facebook group for more information.

Events and races

Twillight Running Festival
QUT Running will be entering teams into various running, and obstacle, races throughout the year. One of the earliest races of the year is the Twilight running festival held at St Lucia on the 20th March. This is a festival with distances suiting everyone; 21.1km, 10km, 5km, 1km.
http://www.twilightrun.com.au/

QUT Classic
QUT Classic is an event organised by the Guild and is usually run in early May. The distances covered are 10km, 5km and 1km.


Brisbane Marathon Festival
Brisbane Marathon Festival is one of Brisbane’s major sporting events. The distances covered are 42.2km, 21.1km, 10km, 5km and the courses are run through the CBD on the 7th of August.
http://www.brisbanemarathon.com/

Bridge To Brisbane
Later on in August (28th), the annual Bridge to Brisbane event occurs. The race distances are 10km and 5km – with the 10km starting at the Gateway Bridge and finishing at the Brisbane RNA Showgrounds. QUT Running always has a strong presence in this event.
http://www.bridgetobrisbane.com.au/

Tough Mudder
As well as running events, QUT running usually enters teams in obstacle races such as Tough Mudder (7th & 8th May) and Spartan Race (27th Feb) also. These events are always a lot of fun and can be taken as seriously or laid-back as you wish.
https://toughmudder.com/

Other Events
There are many other events happing through the year in Brisbane and we will aim to get involved in as many as possible. If you have any suggestions of events you’d like to participate in, let one of our executives know and we can organise a team!

Session Overview

  • Meeting point for all running sessions (including bootcamp) is the Kidney Lawn.
  • Gym and Swimming groups meet in front of the Healthstream entrance.
  • We have the possibility to store bags in X Block if you are showing up on time.
  • The university is providing public showers.

Swimming

Is there any better motivation to start into a new week than to jump into a pool? Of course there isn’t. So come along to our Monday morning swimming session. Meeting point is right in front of the Healthstream entrance next to the food court on Gardens Point Campus. Please be there shortly before 8am, so that you don’t lose too much time getting changed. Using 2 different 50meter swimming lanes, we are providing good exercises and advices for your whole body, depending on your individual level. Full membership is required!

Running

This is our most popular running session. Non regard of your running skills, come along to a fun session through the Botanic Garden. Our running coordinator Christoph is providing many different exercises to help you to improve agility, speed, acceleration and endurance. The sessions will usually have the form of an interval session with varying length and amount of the intervals. Furthermore this is a great opportunity to meet other fellow runners, to have a chat and make friends. If you are new to QUT Running, this is the session you should definitely attend. We are happy to answer questions, explain what’s going on or just introduce you to other people. Meeting point for this session is the Kidney Lawn. From there we head to X-Block to store our bags, before we start our run in the Botanic Garden.

Casual Running

You we’re not able to attend the Monday session? Or you need some extra workouts to stay fit? No worries! This casual running session is suitable for everybody. We’ll take it easier today after our Monday night with QUT Running Social! Starting at 5 pm from Kidney Lawn, we head of to either the Botanic Garden or to a nice paced track along the river.

Gym

Healthstream is providing the opportunity for us to use their gym facilities (including changing room and showers) on Wednesdays from 3pm to 4pm. Meeting point is right in front of the Healthstream entrance next to the food court on Gardens Point Campus. Please be there shortly before 3pm, so that you don’t lose too much time getting changed. During the gym session you are your own boss, we won’t tell you what to do. Create your own workout or take some advices from our experienced gym coordinator Håvard. Full membership is required!

Intermediate and Expert Running

Attention! For experienced runners only! If you’re unsure about being experienced enough; are you able to cover 10km in less than 55min? If the answer is yes, come along! We are trying to push you even harder. A mixture of endurance, mountain, speed or interval sessions is waiting for you. Meeting at Kidney Lawn at 5 pm, we head off to several different tracks throughout Brisbane. You are guided by the experienced runner Eamon Burke.

Beginner Running

Our Beginner session on Thursdays at 5pm is for all those who recently started to run or are just coming back from a longer break. All exercises are designed for beginners! We help you to get into this sport and to motivate you to run more often. We won’t cover a long distance or a high speed. Meeting point is the Kidney Lawn.

Bootcamp

Bootcamp is an excellent opportunity to work on your stamina and strength/stability at the same time. Our bootcamp coordinator Håvard is combining running and strength exercises for you in order to get your body fit for the beach! Every fitness level is welcome. Meeting point is the Kidney Lawn. Come and join us for a nice session in the Botanic Garden.
