About

QUT Running is a social & fitness group for anyone interested in improving their health, fitness and well-being. We are by no means elite runners, but cater to people at any level from beginner to the very experienced. Being a social club we also host a number of events throughout the year, and provide you with the opportunity to meet a variety of new people at QUT!

Social

COVID-19

Due to the ongoing pandemic and restrictions surrounding social distancing, QUT Running has temporarily paused social events following runs.

We hope that these will resume shortly as we monitor government guidelines and updates closely.

Our club has a big social aspect. Following Monday's session, we unwind with dinner or drinks at a venue located within the city. This is a great way to meet new friends thanks to a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. After dinner sometimes we kick on at Music City for karaoke or head to the Down Under bar. On Monday's Down Under Bar host games called Bar Wars which is always massive fun. Every few weeks we spice things up by booking out a couple of lanes at Strike Bowling and playing laser tag, or having pizza and drinks on the lawn at South Bank.

We also attend events hosted by the QUT Guild and co-host events with other clubs, such as QUT Snowriders and QUT Cheer and Dance. These provide excellent opportunities to meet new friends in an a relaxed environment!
