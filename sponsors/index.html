Down Under on Facebook

Brisbane is the perfect place to party, with its superb weather, bars, clubs and pubs, and the Base Backpackers Brisbane Central youth hostel is located in the centre of all the action!

Base Backpackers Brisbane Central has everything a backpacker could want, with our five floors offering a wide variety of rooms to appeal to everyone from the newly-arrived to the newly-wed, including dormitory and private rooms. All rooms are secured via electronic keycards, while you can keep your valuables doubly secured with our supplied lockers. Linen is also provided so there's no need to bring your sleeping bag!

Come on down to Brisbane's most famous backpacker bar where you can win great prizes, dance the night away: or even meet Mr/Mrs Right (or Mr/Mrs Right Now…). If you're after something a little quieter then our Zi Bar does great food and coffee, with regular functions to keep you entertained.
